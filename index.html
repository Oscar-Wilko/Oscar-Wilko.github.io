About Me

I am a graduate of the University Of West Of England accomplishing a first in Bsc Games Technology specialising in the tools of Unity and C# game developement. I achieved the Dean's List award in 2022, was awarded The Outstanding TIGA Graduate of the Year award, and was nominated for TIGA Best Student Game in 2024.

Although I specialise in C# Object Oriented Programming for Unity I have also programmed in C++, Python, HTML, CSS and XAML. Certain tools I have used along the process have been: Unity, Visual Studio, GitHub, Unreal Engine 5, CLion, PyASGE, DirectX11, Pycharm, SFML, Trello, GIMP, Blender, Maya, SketchUp, WPF, and more...
