About The Work

To better expand my capabilities in game developement I decided to branch into an area of minimal knowledge and that was VFX and shaders. The unity shader graph and VFX graph were brand new to me so I researched through multiple examples, tutorials, guides and layout explanations to understand the core workflow of these systems. To which I then exprerimented with the tools until I decided to make one fleshed out effect, which started the creation of the below videos and taught me the knowledge of how to improve and make better effects.
