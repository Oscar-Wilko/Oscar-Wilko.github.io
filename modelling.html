About The Models

For GCSE I used SkecthUp to produce artistic models and architectural designs, this then developed into finding a job that utilised this skill in SketchUp. After years of producing terrains and models for the job, I had built techniques and knowledge of 3D tools, which gave me the idea of going further. To help produce models in the future for game projects I decided to invest my time into learning and utilising Blender. This started with online tutorials and messing around with the software until I decided to focus in on one model. This model being a pre-existing game character, Aurora from League of Legends, this would prove as both reference material and a point of perspective on if the model is well made.
