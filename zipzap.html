About The Game

Project ZipZap is an isometric fast-paced hack-and-slash game produced by me and 10 other university students that is available for free on Steam storepage. Play through 5 levels of rampant difficulty while mastering 3 different dash types that have their own ability and ultimate. The game does come with a small note of story with an initial cutscene and the final level being a boss fight against E-Dog. Our game was nominated for TIGA Awards for "Creative Assembly: Best Game" in 2024 and has positive reviews.

Although I had worked on a wide range of areas both back-end and front-end my most promonent part is the TimeScaleManager which allowed the developers to make certain weapons or effects slow down specific objects or object types.
